Public Assistance Information System

Design
Web App

PAIS is a digital solution aimed at expediting the application process for SNAP / TANF benefits, ensuring that those who require assistance receive it in a timely manner.

Before the Covid-19 pandemic, Hawaii SNAP / TANF applications were solely available in paper format, resulting in a bottleneck for applicants when offices were closed. Within a day of launching the website, over 1000 applications were submitted. I led UI / UX design, and my designs centered around simplifying the application process and improving its accessibility for users. This resulted in a responsive single-column layout and I strategically incorporated bold, eye-catching colors to enhance call-to-action elements.
