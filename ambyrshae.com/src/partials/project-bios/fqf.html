French Quarter Fest App

Navigating crowds through one of the nation's largest free music festival in the south.

Learn more at http://frenchquarterfest.org/get-the-app/

My very first software project was the French Quarter Festival mobile app. I helped develop the Android app natively using Android Studio, Java, Material Design and Espresso testing. I also lead my team in scrum and agile methodologies so that we could meet our 2 month deadline. I then rolled off the project as a developer and became a mentor for 3 years. I guided interns on development, design and project management.
