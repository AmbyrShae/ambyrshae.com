BENEFITS ELIGIBILITY SYSTEM

Streamlining the eligibility process across Hawaiian public assistance programs in order to provide services more quickly.

Learn more at https://humanservices.hawaii.gov/bessd/

I incorporated scrum / agile methodologies in order to improve team productivity. I also led designs for Release 0.7 and worked closely with product owners and dev team in order to meet our deadlines. I used iterative design to incorporate changes to enhance the application. All designs were done using Adobe XD.
