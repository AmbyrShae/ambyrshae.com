BENEFITS ELIGIBILITY SYSTEM

Streamlining the eligibility process across Hawaiian public assistance programs in order to provide services more quickly. Learn more at https://humanservices.hawaii.gov/bessd/

I led designs for Release 0.5 and worked closely with product owners and dev team in order to meet our deadlines. I used iterative design to incorporate changes to enhance the application. All designs were done using Adobe XD and were shared with a UI team via cloud in order to co-edit.
